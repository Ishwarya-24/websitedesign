Environmental Monitoring Systems


Soil Moisture Sensing

For both landscape and agricultural application soil moisture sensors increase the efficiency of irrigation systems by preventing over-watering and leaching of fertilizers. Improved management of soil moisture allows farmers and urban landscapers not only to use less water on crops, lawns, and gardens, but also to see improvements in plant health growth and yield throughout critical growth stages and more extreme climate conditions.
