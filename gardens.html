GARDENS BY THE BAY


From major 3D pump station design, to open plan
presentation turf zones with intricate botanical
gardens, to micro sub-surface drip irrigation
delivering precise quantities of nutrient enriched
water for all plant species from around the world
in on ground and vertical planted structures.
Virtually every aspect of irrigation products and current
design trends have been utilised in this project.

This extensive project called for the latest
design techniques and product selection to make this
project come together. Our Singapore and Australian
teams are proud to be playing a role in
this visionary project.
