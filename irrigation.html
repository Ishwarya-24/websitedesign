Gardens By The Bay - Irrigation


Gardens by the Bay are a high quality botanical
gardens located at the mouth of the Singapore River.
The Gardens consist of a 1.2 Ha cool dry and
a .8 Ha cool moist conservatory, 23 supertrees and
superb gardens. The irrigation system comprises of 5 pump
stations, 5 central computer control systems, 340 solenoid
valves and 2,800 sprinklers.
