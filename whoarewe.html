What we do

Water Equipment Technology (WET), a Queensland, Australia & Singapore based company, has earned an industry reputation in maintaining high standards in design, construction oversight & delivery for pumping and irrigation systems. We offer a complete water management solution from concept stage, scheme and detailed design & planning all the way through to construction & project management. Our objective is to work within the environment with zero tolerance towards harming it. In fact, being water focused, our activities enhance and protect the environment while supporting business goals. Our abilities have been recognised by a steady stream of clients in Local & State Government, commerce, mining industry, education, sport & recreation.

Who we are

The staff at WET have eighty years consultancy experience between them, over thirty five years in the industry which gives us the ability to provide new and innovative solutions to the most complex irrigation, pumping and water management projects and the best level of service in the market place.
