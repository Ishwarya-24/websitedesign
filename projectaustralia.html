Mt Coo-tha Botanic Gardens - Brisbane


A sustainable water strategy with carried
out on this site. This report was to ascertain
the water harvesting capability based on catchment
area and rainfall, the size of storage required
for a sustainable water supply. The calculated
storage is 65mL within 3 catchments. A 250mm
transfer system complete with pumps is required
to move water between storage locations.

Client: Moreton Bay Regional Council.
Main Con: Scape Shapes.
Size: 3 Ha.
