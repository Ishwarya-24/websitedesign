Gardens By The Bay - Pumping

Gardens by the Bay are a high quality botanical
gardens located at the mouth of the Singapore River.
The irrigation system comprises of 5 pump stations
each with its own sand media filtration system.
The 5 submersible pumping systems are located in
a wet well pumping water from the lake.
